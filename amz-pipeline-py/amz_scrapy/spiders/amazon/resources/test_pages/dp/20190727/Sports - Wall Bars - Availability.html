4.0 out of 5 starsGreat gym but beware of wall mounting!!!
December 13, 2018
Verified Purchase
I dont write reviews often but felt I shoud leave feedback about this gym. First of all, my son loves it! As far as the quality, its a great product. The pieces are all very sturdy and well made. That being said, I have to give a HUGE thumbs down to the instructions. WORST INSTRUCTIONS EVER! Just pictures with lines and arrows. Ive assembled a lot of furniture in my life and I can read picture instructions but these were so confusing! It took way too long to put this thing together.

The most disappointing part was the wall mounting pieces. The kit includes dry wall screws and long plastic pieces to secure the screws. But theyre too long! They go far beyond the drywall. Drywall isnt very thick so these go past the drywall into the empty space beyond which makes them useless. When I pulled on the gym to test its steadiness, it pulled right away from the wall.

My husband and I ended up making a trip to Home Depot. This gym is 31 inches wide. Standard wall studs are placed 24 inches apart in a wall so theres no way to secure this gym to studs on both sides. We had 2x4s cut to 36 inches and secured those to the studs then secured the gym to the 2x4s. The photos show how we did this. Now the whole structure is literally a part of the wall. It wont come down unless the entire wall comes down but that could not happen with the wall mounting accessories included. They are completely inadequate and NOT at all safe for your child. Find a better way to mount this or it WILL fall and could really hurt someone.

Some of the holes were not drilled evenly either so the top portion and bottom portion of the ladder did not line up exactly. I also show this in the photos. I was able to tighten them together but theyre a bit crooked.

Other than the poor mounting materials and the frustration with the instructions, were happy with it. My 5 year old has climbed all over it and its sturdy. Good product but cant give 5 stars.
