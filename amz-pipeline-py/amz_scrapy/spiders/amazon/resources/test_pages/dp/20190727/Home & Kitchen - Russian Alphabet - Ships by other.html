Product description

This interactive Russian language electronic poster lets your child study the alphabet and learn to read by teaching them letters and sounds, singing songs, reciting poems, taking quizzes, and even complimenting them on good work. This new edition has 8 modes: On/Off, Study, Songs, Sounds, Test, Game, Numbers and Poems. Playing with this poster develops visual thinking, vocabulary, and speech.
